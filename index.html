About Me

Baby Steps

I love computers since I can remember so I’m finally pursuing my Tech dreams. My dad used to bring these massive boxes of software from work and I would wait to see whether it was a game or some old app. It became my dream to create beautiful apps and to make them as functional as possible.

While looking for Tech Courses, I’ve found CareerFoundry’s 6-month Full-Stack Web Development course, and the experience couldn’t have been better!

 
 
Adult Life

In my previous role, my key tasks were to follow the aircraft safety procedures, and secure the aircraft before the passengers came in. The direct and continuous communication with the flight deck to maintain the passengers safe and relaxed, helped me improve my multi-tasking skills.

I learned that the best way to communicate with people is through empathy and patience. I love to meet and connect with new people, and I believe there’s always room for personal growth and improvement.

Music Production

I started to learn how to produce music online when I was 18 years old, I've been producing for more than 10 years, it's one of my biggest passions.

Usually, I produce deep and melodic electronic music, but in general, I have a very eclectic taste and I listen to everything from Hip-Hop to Rock music.

 
 
What am I looking for?

After obtaining my User Experience degree, the natural move was always Web Development. I'm very passionate when it comes to UX Design also. Seeking opportunities in the Web Development field, or perhaps a mix between Coding and UX!

My product building preferences are apps/websites that are aimed at improving the user’s everyday lives, but I’m open to different challenges!
