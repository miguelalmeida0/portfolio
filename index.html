About Me




Hi, I’m Miguel. Seeking opportunities in the web development field. Very passionate when it comes to UX Design also. After obtaining my User Experience degree, the natural move was always Web Development.

I've acquired knowledge on how the user’s brain works and interacts with an Interface, deeply fascinated with how to create those same interfaces. I went from understanding the User Experience, to building that experience!



Had more than 3000 flights. Trained around 30 cabin crew newcomers, explaining to them the daily procedures, when it comes to safety, how to approach passengers or de-escalate possible violent situations.

On some days I was around 1000 people per day! These interactions taught me how to work for the user, applied to proper designs and functionalities in a website, how it should look, and how it should function.

The truth is that I love computers since I can remember. My dad used to bring these massive boxes of software from work and I would wait to see whether it was a game or some old app. It became my dream to create beautiful apps.




The main goal is to join a company that provides great onboarding and mentorship.



Fluent in HTML, CSS, Javascript, React and Node.js.
